Our commitment
OUR ETHICS
STATEMENT.
Your work is always your own

We never complete work on behalf of our students. We suggest, proofread, and help refine — but we never write what should be written by you.

We spare no effort

Your work goes through as many rounds of feedback as needed. We will do everything possible to make it outstanding and maximise your chances.

We hold ourselves to the same standard

Multiple expert reviews, always

How we work
THE PROCESS.

You will work on your project and application for months — in some cases, years. We structure that time to make it purposeful, manageable, and effective, in parallel with your exams and school activities.

01
Topic & Format Selection

Work with Ariton consultants to identify the right project for you — at the intersection of your interests, your long-term goals, and a cause or question that is worth pursuing.

02
Strategy & Planning

Whether you are publishing research, raising funds, building a community, or launching an initiative — we structure a long-term plan that fits your academic calendar and commitments.

03
Execution & Mentorship

Our consultants bring extensive project management, research, and entrepreneurship experience. We guide every step, keep you on track, and review every milestone.

04
Managing Parallel Commitments

Ariton consultants make sure you are on top of all tasks involved — so you complete your project and applications without letting either fall short.

05
Essay & Portfolio Integration

Upon completion, we ensure your achievements and reflections are captured in your college application — from Passion Project reports to Common App and UCAS essays.

06
Final Review & Submission

Every application is reviewed by Sofia and at least two additional consultants before submission. Then interview coaching, waitlist strategy, and decision guidance until the end.

When should I start preparing for university applications? +
We suggest Year 9 or 10, but even Year 11 is not too late. If you are unsure, get in touch and let's discuss your situation — we will work out a long-term strategy to get you to your dream university.
There is so much to do — where do I even start? +
Start with a free consultation. We will assess your current profile, academic standing, and goals — and give you a clear picture of exactly what to prioritise and in what order. You will leave with a concrete direction.
Which universities does Ariton specialise in? +
We specialise in the most selective institutions in the US and UK — Ivy League, Oxbridge, UCL, LSE, Imperial, and top liberal arts colleges. Sofia has direct, first-hand experience of both systems, and all mentors hold degrees from these institutions.
I am already going into Year 12 — is it too late? +
No. Year 12 is still a strong starting point for US applications. For UK applications, earlier is always better — but we have successfully supported Year 12 students for Oxbridge entry. Get in touch and we will tell you honestly what is achievable.
How does Ariton approach Passion Projects? +
We guide students through every stage: topic selection, format, long-term project strategy, execution, and ultimately integration into the college application portfolio. Crucially, your work is always your own — we advise, review, and challenge, but we never write for you.
Do you work with international students? +
Yes. We serve students in Korea, Singapore, and internationally. Sessions are conducted online or in person at our Seoul and Singapore locations. Our London network supports UK-based students.
